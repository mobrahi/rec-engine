Prediction Formula

Prediction for Customer u, Product i:

P(u,i) = Σv∈N(u) Sim(u,v) · rv,i / Σv∈N(u) |Sim(u,v)|

Where N(u) is the set of K nearest neighbors, Sim(u,v) is the similarity between customers, and rv,i is the rating.
